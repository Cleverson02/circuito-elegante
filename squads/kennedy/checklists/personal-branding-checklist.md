# Personal Branding Checklist

> Agent: kennedy-brand
> Source: No B.S. Brand-Building by Direct Response — Personal Brand Model
> Version: 1.0
> Use: When building a personal brand that generates revenue through DR

---

## Pre-Requisites
- [ ] Area of expertise defined
- [ ] Target audience for personal brand identified
- [ ] Willingness to be visible and polarizing
- [ ] Content creation capacity assessed

---

## STEP 1: Authority Positioning
- [ ] Book published or in development (the ultimate authority marker)
- [ ] Speaking engagements active (paid or promotional)
- [ ] Media appearances or contributions
- [ ] Published articles in industry publications
- [ ] Podcast, YouTube channel, or content platform active

## STEP 2: Celebrity Factor
- [ ] Polarizing opinions shared publicly
- [ ] Personal stories and vulnerabilities used
- [ ] Personality is part of the brand (not hidden behind corporate)
- [ ] Recognizable visual brand (consistent imagery, style)
- [ ] Signature phrases or catchphrases developed

## STEP 3: Expert Model Integration
- [ ] Personal brand drives revenue (not just reputation)
- [ ] Products and services connected to the personal brand
- [ ] Speaking leads to consulting or product sales
- [ ] Content marketing drives lead generation
- [ ] Personal brand creates premium pricing power

## STEP 4: DR Integration
- [ ] Every personal brand touchpoint has a response mechanism
- [ ] Personal brand activities generate leads (not just applause)
- [ ] ROI tracked for personal brand investments
- [ ] Personal brand content captures contact info

---

## Scoring
- 16-18/18 = Strong personal brand generating revenue
- 12-15/18 = Good foundation — activate missing elements
- 8-11/18 = Early stage — build authority and visibility
- Below 8 = No personal brand yet — start with Step 1
