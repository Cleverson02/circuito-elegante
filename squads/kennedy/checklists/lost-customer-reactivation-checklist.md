# Lost Customer Reactivation Checklist

> Agent: kennedy-referrals
> Source: The Ultimate No BS Referral Machine — Reactivation System
> Version: 1.0
> Use: When designing campaigns to win back lapsed or lost customers

---

## Pre-Requisites
- [ ] Lost/lapsed customers identified (inactive 90+ days)
- [ ] Customer contact information current
- [ ] Reason for departure understood (if possible)
- [ ] Special comeback offer designed

---

## STEP 1: List Preparation
- [ ] Lapsed customers segmented by value (high, medium, low)
- [ ] Lapsed customers segmented by recency (90 days, 6 months, 1 year+)
- [ ] Contact information verified and updated
- [ ] Previous purchase history documented
- [ ] Reason for departure noted (if known)

## STEP 2: The Reactivation Letter
- [ ] Personal tone ("We noticed you haven't been around...")
- [ ] Acknowledgment that something may have gone wrong
- [ ] No blame — takes responsibility and offers olive branch
- [ ] Specific, generous comeback offer
- [ ] Deadline for the comeback offer
- [ ] Multiple response channels included

## STEP 3: The Comeback Offer
- [ ] Genuinely special offer (not available to new customers)
- [ ] Discount, bonus, or VIP treatment included
- [ ] Offer addresses likely reason for departure
- [ ] Risk reversal stronger than normal (they are skeptical)
- [ ] Limited-time availability creates urgency

## STEP 4: Multi-Touch Sequence
- [ ] Touch 1: Reactivation letter (physical mail preferred)
- [ ] Touch 2: Follow-up email (3-5 days after mail)
- [ ] Touch 3: Phone call (for high-value customers)
- [ ] Touch 4: Final "last chance" message
- [ ] Each touch adds new angle or incentive

## STEP 5: Re-Onboarding
- [ ] Reactivated customers get special welcome back
- [ ] Cause of departure addressed proactively
- [ ] Extra attention paid during first 30 days back
- [ ] Feedback collected on why they returned
- [ ] Retention plan activated immediately

---

## Scoring
- 22-24/24 = Comprehensive reactivation campaign — deploy
- 18-21/24 = Strong campaign — fill gaps
- 12-17/24 = Partial campaign — missing key elements
- Below 12 = Not a reactivation campaign yet — build from Step 1
