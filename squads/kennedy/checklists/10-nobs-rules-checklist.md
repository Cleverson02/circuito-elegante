# 10 No B.S. Rules of Direct Marketing Checklist

> Agent: kennedy-direct-response
> Source: No B.S. Direct Marketing (3rd Ed, 2018) — 10 Non-Negotiable Rules
> Version: 1.0
> Use: When auditing any marketing piece or campaign for DR compliance

---

## Pre-Requisites
- [ ] Marketing piece or campaign ready for review
- [ ] Target market defined
- [ ] Tracking mechanisms available

---

## Rule 1: There Will Always Be an Offer
- [ ] A clear, specific offer is present
- [ ] The offer is compelling (not just "call us" or "visit our website")
- [ ] The offer gives a reason to respond NOW
- [ ] The offer has a tangible value proposition
- [ ] Score: ___/5

## Rule 2: There Will Be a Reason to Respond Right Now
- [ ] A deadline is stated (specific date or limited quantity)
- [ ] Consequence of delay is clear (price goes up, bonuses disappear)
- [ ] Urgency is REAL, not manufactured
- [ ] Fast-action bonus or incentive included
- [ ] Score: ___/5

## Rule 3: You Will Give Clear Instructions
- [ ] Prospect knows EXACTLY what to do next
- [ ] Step-by-step instructions provided ("Call this number NOW")
- [ ] Multiple response channels offered (phone, web, mail)
- [ ] Instructions repeated at least twice in the piece
- [ ] Score: ___/5

## Rule 4: There Will Be Tracking and Measurement
- [ ] Unique phone number, URL, or code assigned to this piece
- [ ] Source tracking in place (knows which piece generated the response)
- [ ] ROI can be calculated per piece/campaign
- [ ] Results database maintained
- [ ] Score: ___/5

## Rule 5: Branding Will Be Built by Meaningful Follow-Up
- [ ] Follow-up sequence designed (not one-and-done)
- [ ] Multi-step follow-up planned (minimum 3 touches)
- [ ] Follow-up adds value, not just "checking in"
- [ ] Brand is built through repeated DR contact, not institutional ads
- [ ] Score: ___/5

## Rule 6: Results Rule — Not Pretty Awards
- [ ] Success measured by revenue and ROI, not awards or "likes"
- [ ] Ugly that works beats beautiful that doesn't
- [ ] No institutional vanity metrics (impressions, brand awareness)
- [ ] Only metric that matters: did it generate profitable response?
- [ ] Score: ___/5

## Rule 7: You Will Be a Tough-Minded Disciplinarian
- [ ] Every dollar spent on marketing is accountable
- [ ] No sacred cows (everything gets tested and measured)
- [ ] Underperforming campaigns are killed quickly
- [ ] Winners are scaled aggressively
- [ ] Score: ___/5

## Rule 8: You Will Have a Thick Skin
- [ ] Willing to be aggressive in marketing (not apologetic)
- [ ] Not worried about what peers think (worried about what customers do)
- [ ] Willing to follow up persistently
- [ ] Not deterred by complaints from non-buyers
- [ ] Score: ___/5

## Rule 9: You Will Be in the Market
- [ ] Studying what is working for others in DR
- [ ] Swipe file maintained and growing
- [ ] Attending DR-focused events and groups
- [ ] Constantly consuming direct mail (even others' mail)
- [ ] Score: ___/5

## Rule 10: You Will Be Willing to Be "Bad"
- [ ] Marketing is NOT sanitized to avoid any criticism
- [ ] Willing to send "ugly" mail that gets response
- [ ] Willing to follow up aggressively
- [ ] Willing to use fear, urgency, and emotional triggers
- [ ] Willing to fire bad customers
- [ ] Score: ___/5
