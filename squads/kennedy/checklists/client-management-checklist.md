# Copywriting Client Management Checklist

> Agent: kennedy-copy
> Source: Copywriting Academy — Client Management System
> Version: 1.0
> Use: When managing copywriting clients and projects

---

## Pre-Requisites
- [ ] Client agreement or scope of work defined
- [ ] Project timeline established
- [ ] Deliverables clearly specified
- [ ] Payment terms agreed

---

## STEP 1: Client Onboarding

### 1.1 Information Gathering
- [ ] Client questionnaire completed (business, market, product)
- [ ] Target audience profile provided by client
- [ ] Existing marketing materials collected
- [ ] Competitor samples gathered
- [ ] Client's voice and brand guidelines reviewed

### 1.2 Expectation Setting
- [ ] Timeline communicated and agreed
- [ ] Revision policy stated (number of rounds included)
- [ ] Communication cadence established (when and how)
- [ ] Deliverable format specified
- [ ] Payment milestones defined

---

## STEP 2: Project Execution

### 2.1 Research Phase
- [ ] Market research conducted
- [ ] Prospect interviews or surveys reviewed (if available)
- [ ] Swipe file researched (what's working in the industry)
- [ ] Key messaging angles identified
- [ ] USP articulated or refined

### 2.2 Draft Phase
- [ ] First draft follows Kennedy's DR principles
- [ ] Headline bank created (minimum 20 options)
- [ ] Body copy follows appropriate structure (28-step if sales letter)
- [ ] Fascination bullets included
- [ ] Offer section clearly constructed

### 2.3 Review Phase
- [ ] Internal review completed before client sees it
- [ ] Copy checked against all DR compliance standards
- [ ] Spelling, grammar, and consistency verified
- [ ] All claims supported by proof elements

---

## STEP 3: Client Presentation

### 3.1 Delivery
- [ ] Copy delivered on time (or early)
- [ ] Explanation document included (why specific choices were made)
- [ ] Headline rationale provided
- [ ] Key sections highlighted for client attention
- [ ] Revision instructions clear

### 3.2 Revision Management
- [ ] Client feedback organized and addressed systematically
- [ ] Revisions that strengthen DR compliance accepted readily
- [ ] Revisions that weaken copy addressed diplomatically
- [ ] Documentation of what changed and why
- [ ] Revised version delivered within agreed timeline

---

## STEP 4: Project Completion

### 4.1 Final Delivery
- [ ] Final version approved by client
- [ ] All file formats delivered as specified
- [ ] Usage rights and ownership transferred
- [ ] Final invoice sent
- [ ] Testimonial requested after results come in

### 4.2 Results Tracking
- [ ] Follow up with client for results data
- [ ] Add results to portfolio/case studies (with permission)
- [ ] Lessons learned documented
- [ ] Upsell opportunity identified (what else do they need?)

---

## Validation
- [ ] Client onboarding was thorough (no missing information)
- [ ] Copy delivered on time and to spec
- [ ] All DR principles applied
- [ ] Client relationship maintained for future work
- [ ] Results tracked and documented

---

## Scoring
- 26-30/30 = Professional copywriting operation — maintain standards
- 20-25/30 = Good process — tighten weak areas
- 14-19/30 = Process gaps causing issues — formalize procedures
- Below 14 = Ad hoc project management — systematize immediately
