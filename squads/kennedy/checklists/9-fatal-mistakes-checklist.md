# 9 Fatal Marketing Mistakes Checklist

> Agent: kennedy-magnetic
> Source: Magnetic Marketing (2018) — 9 Fatal Marketing Mistakes
> Version: 1.0
> Use: When diagnosing why marketing is not producing results

---

## Pre-Requisites
- [ ] Current marketing materials and campaigns gathered
- [ ] Business revenue and marketing spend documented
- [ ] Honest self-assessment willingness

---

## Mistake 1: Being Boring
- [ ] Marketing creates an emotional reaction (any reaction)
- [ ] Headlines are provocative, not predictable
- [ ] Copy tells stories, not just lists features
- [ ] Marketing would NOT put a prospect to sleep
- [ ] Pattern interrupt exists (something unexpected)
- Status: [ ] GUILTY / [ ] NOT GUILTY

## Mistake 2: Wasting Money on Brand/Image Advertising
- [ ] Every marketing piece has a DIRECT response mechanism
- [ ] No "awareness" campaigns without measurable ROI
- [ ] No ads that just show the logo and phone number
- [ ] Every dollar spent is trackable to a response
- [ ] Institutional advertising has been eliminated or minimized
- Status: [ ] GUILTY / [ ] NOT GUILTY

## Mistake 3: Having No USP
- [ ] Clear Unique Selling Proposition defined
- [ ] USP differentiates from ALL competitors
- [ ] USP is specific and provable
- [ ] USP appears in every marketing piece
- [ ] Cannot be easily copied by competitors
- Status: [ ] GUILTY / [ ] NOT GUILTY

## Mistake 4: No Follow-Up System
- [ ] Leads are followed up within 24 hours
- [ ] Multi-step follow-up sequence in place (minimum 7 touches)
- [ ] Follow-up is automated (not dependent on memory)
- [ ] Unconverted leads continue to receive contact
- [ ] Follow-up includes multiple media channels
- Status: [ ] GUILTY / [ ] NOT GUILTY

## Mistake 5: No Compelling Offer
- [ ] Marketing has a specific, time-sensitive offer
- [ ] Offer provides genuine value to the prospect
- [ ] Offer includes risk reversal (guarantee)
- [ ] Offer gives reason to act NOW
- [ ] Offer is not just "free consultation" or "call for info"
- Status: [ ] GUILTY / [ ] NOT GUILTY

## Mistake 6: No Customer Retention Strategy
- [ ] Active system for keeping customers buying
- [ ] Monthly newsletter or regular communication in place
- [ ] Customer appreciation program exists
- [ ] Loyalty program or membership in place
- [ ] Lost customer reactivation system active
- Status: [ ] GUILTY / [ ] NOT GUILTY

## Mistake 7: Wrong Target Market
- [ ] Target market is specific (not "everyone")
- [ ] Marketing speaks to ONE defined audience
- [ ] Market has ability AND willingness to pay
- [ ] Market is accessible through available media
- [ ] "Starving crowd" has been identified
- Status: [ ] GUILTY / [ ] NOT GUILTY

## Mistake 8: Failure to Be Different
- [ ] Business looks different from competitors
- [ ] Marketing looks different from competitors
- [ ] Customer experience is different from competitors
- [ ] Would not blend in at an industry trade show
- [ ] Something truly proprietary exists
- Status: [ ] GUILTY / [ ] NOT GUILTY

## Mistake 9: Failure to Measure
- [ ] Know exactly what each new customer costs to acquire
- [ ] Know lifetime customer value
- [ ] Every campaign is tracked with unique codes
- [ ] ROI calculated for every marketing channel
- [ ] Unprofitable channels are cut quickly
- Status: [ ] GUILTY / [ ] NOT GUILTY
