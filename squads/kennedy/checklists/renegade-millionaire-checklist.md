# Renegade Millionaire Mindset Checklist

> Agent: kennedy-advisor
> Source: Renegade Millionaire Course — Contrarian Wealth Principles
> Version: 1.0
> Use: When assessing entrepreneurial mindset and wealth-building foundations

---

## Pre-Requisites
- [ ] Willingness to question conventional business wisdom
- [ ] Honest self-assessment of current habits and beliefs
- [ ] Business financials available

---

## PRINCIPLE 1: Think Differently Than the Herd
- [ ] Actively questioning industry "best practices"
- [ ] Not following trends because everyone else is
- [ ] Seeking out contrarian perspectives and information
- [ ] Willing to go against popular opinion when evidence supports it
- [ ] Not seeking approval from peers for business decisions

## PRINCIPLE 2: Time is Your Most Valuable Asset
- [ ] Time vampires identified and eliminated
- [ ] Calendar controlled (not reactive to others' demands)
- [ ] Productive time protected ruthlessly
- [ ] Meetings minimized and structured
- [ ] Delegation active for tasks below your pay grade

## PRINCIPLE 3: Marketing is the #1 Job
- [ ] Personal involvement in marketing (not fully delegated)
- [ ] Marketing studied and practiced continuously
- [ ] Marketing budget treated as investment, not expense
- [ ] DR marketing prioritized over institutional
- [ ] Customer acquisition cost and LTV known

## PRINCIPLE 4: Multiple Income Streams
- [ ] Not dependent on single income source
- [ ] At least 3 income streams active or in development
- [ ] Passive/semi-passive income being built
- [ ] Income diversification across different types (earned, investment, royalty)
- [ ] Each stream evaluated for scalability

## PRINCIPLE 5: Guard Your Mental Environment
- [ ] News and negativity consumption limited
- [ ] Surrounding yourself with ambitious, successful people
- [ ] Reading and learning daily
- [ ] Toxic relationships minimized or eliminated
- [ ] Mental environment supports growth mindset

## PRINCIPLE 6: Speed of Implementation
- [ ] Good ideas executed within 72 hours (not "someday")
- [ ] Analysis paralysis identified and overcome
- [ ] Action beats perfection (launch, then improve)
- [ ] "Ready, Fire, Aim" mentality embraced
- [ ] Velocity of implementation tracked

## PRINCIPLE 7: Charge Premium Prices
- [ ] Not competing on price (competing on value)
- [ ] Prices above industry average
- [ ] Price increases implemented regularly
- [ ] Low-value customers graduated or fired
- [ ] Premium positioning supported by marketing and delivery

## PRINCIPLE 8: Invest in Yourself
- [ ] Annual self-investment budget (coaching, courses, masterminds)
- [ ] Learning from the best in your field (not free content only)
- [ ] Attending high-level events and groups
- [ ] Health and energy actively managed
- [ ] Personal development is a business expense, not a luxury

## PRINCIPLE 9: Build Assets, Not Just Income
- [ ] Building intellectual property (books, courses, systems)
- [ ] Building customer lists and relationships
- [ ] Building brand equity through DR marketing
- [ ] Building systems that work without you
- [ ] Exit strategy considered (even if not imminent)

## PRINCIPLE 10: Be Disciplined
- [ ] Daily routines and rituals established
- [ ] Commitments kept (to yourself and others)
- [ ] Emotional decisions recognized and managed
- [ ] Long-term thinking applied to short-term decisions
- [ ] Self-discipline practiced as the master virtue

---

## Validation
- [ ] Honest assessment completed (no self-deception)
- [ ] Top 3 weakest principles identified
- [ ] Action plan for each weak principle created
- [ ] Review scheduled (quarterly minimum)
- [ ] Accountability partner or group in place

---

## Scoring
- 45-50/50 = Renegade Millionaire mindset — maintain and refine
- 36-44/50 = Strong entrepreneurial foundation — fill the gaps
- 26-35/50 = Significant conventional thinking remaining
- Below 26 = Employee mindset — major paradigm shift needed
