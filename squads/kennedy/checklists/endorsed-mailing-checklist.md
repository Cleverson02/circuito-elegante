# Endorsed Mailing Checklist

> Agent: kennedy-referrals
> Source: The Ultimate No BS Referral Machine — Endorsed Mailing System
> Version: 1.0
> Use: When setting up endorsed mailings through existing customer or partner lists

---

## Pre-Requisites
- [ ] Endorser identified (customer, partner, or ally with a list)
- [ ] Endorser's relationship with their list is strong
- [ ] Offer designed specifically for this endorsed list
- [ ] Tracking mechanism ready

---

## STEP 1: Endorser Selection
- [ ] Endorser has trust and credibility with their audience
- [ ] Endorser's audience overlaps with your target market
- [ ] Endorser willing to put their name and reputation on the mailing
- [ ] Endorser benefits from the arrangement (incentive, reciprocity)
- [ ] Endorser's list is active and responsive (not cold/dead list)

## STEP 2: The Endorsed Letter
- [ ] Letter written IN the endorser's voice (sounds like them)
- [ ] Personal recommendation from endorser (not generic)
- [ ] Endorser shares their personal experience with your product/service
- [ ] Endorser explains WHY they are recommending this
- [ ] Letter includes specific call to action
- [ ] Endorser's signature on the letter

## STEP 3: The Offer
- [ ] Special offer exclusive to this endorsed list
- [ ] Offer references the endorser ("because [Name] sent you...")
- [ ] Offer has genuine value beyond what you normally offer
- [ ] Deadline or scarcity mechanism in place
- [ ] Response mechanism is trackable to this specific mailing

## STEP 4: Production and Delivery
- [ ] Mailing list provided by endorser (or mailed by endorser)
- [ ] Letter mailed in endorser's envelope/letterhead
- [ ] Real stamps used (A-Pile compliance)
- [ ] Timing coordinated with endorser (avoid conflicts)
- [ ] Follow-up sequence ready for respondents

## STEP 5: Relationship Management
- [ ] Endorser receives compensation or reciprocal endorsement
- [ ] Results shared with endorser transparently
- [ ] Endorser's list is respected (no spam, no list theft)
- [ ] Ongoing relationship maintained for future endorsements
- [ ] Thank-you sent to endorser regardless of results

---

## Validation
- [ ] Endorsed letter reads authentically (sounds like the endorser)
- [ ] Offer is genuinely special for this audience
- [ ] Tracking in place to measure response
- [ ] Endorser has reviewed and approved the final mailing
- [ ] Follow-up ready for respondents

---

## Scoring
- 24-26/26 = Excellent endorsed mailing — send with confidence
- 20-23/26 = Strong mailing — minor refinements
- 14-19/26 = Needs work — key elements missing
- Below 14 = Not yet an endorsed mailing — too generic
