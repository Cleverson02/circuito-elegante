# Advertorial Writing Checklist

> Agent: kennedy-copy
> Source: Copywriting Academy — Advertorial System
> Version: 1.0
> Use: When creating advertorials (ads disguised as editorial content)

---

## Pre-Requisites
- [ ] Target publication or platform identified
- [ ] Product/service with a compelling story
- [ ] Editorial style of the publication studied
- [ ] Legal compliance confirmed (FTC disclosure if required)

---

## STEP 1: Format Matching
- [ ] Looks and reads like an article (not an ad)
- [ ] Typography matches the surrounding editorial content
- [ ] Layout mirrors the publication's article format
- [ ] No obvious "advertisement" signals in design
- [ ] "ADVERTORIAL" or "SPONSORED" label included where required

## STEP 2: Headline
- [ ] Headline reads like a news or feature headline
- [ ] Not salesy or promotional in tone
- [ ] Creates genuine curiosity or interest
- [ ] Would fit naturally on the publication's front page

## STEP 3: Content Quality
- [ ] Provides genuine educational or entertainment value
- [ ] Story arc with beginning, middle, and end
- [ ] Specific details and data points included
- [ ] Expert quotes or references (even self-referencing)
- [ ] Reader learns something useful regardless of buying

## STEP 4: Selling Integration
- [ ] Selling is woven naturally into the story
- [ ] Product/service introduced as part of the narrative
- [ ] Benefits demonstrated through story (not listed)
- [ ] Call to action feels like a natural conclusion
- [ ] Transition from content to offer is seamless

## STEP 5: Response Mechanism
- [ ] Clear next step for interested readers
- [ ] Tracking mechanism unique to this advertorial
- [ ] Response channel matches the publication audience
- [ ] Offer specific to this advertorial (trackable)
- [ ] Multiple response options provided

---

## Scoring
- 22-24/24 = Excellent advertorial — publish with confidence
- 18-21/24 = Strong — minor adjustments for editorial match
- 12-17/24 = Needs work — too salesy or too editorial (balance)
- Below 12 = Not yet an advertorial — either a pure ad or pure article
