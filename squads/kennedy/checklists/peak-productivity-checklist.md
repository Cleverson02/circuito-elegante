# Peak Productivity System Checklist

> Agent: kennedy-advisor
> Source: Renegade Millionaire + Ultimate No B.S. Business Success — Productivity Mastery
> Version: 1.0
> Use: When optimizing personal and business productivity

---

## Pre-Requisites
- [ ] Current work schedule documented
- [ ] Hourly value calculated
- [ ] Time vampires identified
- [ ] Goals and priorities established

---

## STEP 1: Time Architecture
- [ ] Peak productive hours identified and protected
- [ ] Calendar time-blocked (not just task-listed)
- [ ] Revenue-generating activities have dedicated daily blocks
- [ ] "No meeting" zones established
- [ ] Buffer time built in for unexpected demands

## STEP 2: Environment Optimization
- [ ] Workspace designed for focus (minimal distractions)
- [ ] Phone on silent during productive blocks
- [ ] Email checking limited to scheduled times (2-3x/day max)
- [ ] Door closed or availability signals in place
- [ ] Tools and resources within reach (no searching)

## STEP 3: Delegation System
- [ ] Tasks below hourly value identified
- [ ] Delegation plan active (who handles what)
- [ ] Standard operating procedures for delegated tasks
- [ ] Quality checks for delegated work
- [ ] Gradually increasing delegation as systems prove

## STEP 4: Decision Speed
- [ ] Quick decisions on non-critical matters (2-minute rule)
- [ ] Decision frameworks established for recurring choices
- [ ] Perfectionism recognized and managed
- [ ] "Good enough now" beats "perfect later"
- [ ] Implementation speed tracked

## STEP 5: Energy Management
- [ ] Sleep schedule optimized
- [ ] Exercise routine in place
- [ ] Nutrition supports energy (not depletes it)
- [ ] Breaks scheduled between intense work blocks
- [ ] Mental environment curated (positive inputs)

---

## Scoring
- 22-24/24 = Peak productivity — maintain discipline
- 18-21/24 = Strong productivity — optimize weak areas
- 12-17/24 = Significant leaks — implement missing systems
- Below 12 = Productivity crisis — restructure immediately
