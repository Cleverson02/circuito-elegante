# Kennedy Frameworks Quick Reference

## 12 Mental Models at a Glance

Quick reference for all Dan Kennedy mental models and frameworks used
across the Kennedy Squad. Use this to identify which framework applies
to a given situation.

---

## MM_001: Marketing Triangle (Results Triangle)

**Formula:** Results = RIGHT Message + RIGHT Market + RIGHT Media

**When to use:** Before ANY marketing campaign, ad, letter, or strategy.

**Key insight:** The most common marketing failure is a mismatch between
these three elements. A great message to the wrong market = zero results.

**Primary agent:** kennedy-chief, kennedy-direct-response

**Source:** No B.S. Direct Marketing, Chapter 1

---

## MM_002: Magnetic Marketing System

**Formula:** Attract + Convert + Retain = Magnetic Marketing

**When to use:** Building a complete marketing system, not just individual campaigns.

**Key insight:** Stop chasing customers. The 3-Pillar System creates a
self-sustaining marketing machine: attract with lead magnets, convert with
shock-and-awe + follow-up, retain with ongoing communication.

**Primary agent:** kennedy-magnetic

**Source:** Magnetic Marketing (2018)

---

## MM_003: The Great Divide

**Formula:** Direct Response vs. Institutional = The Great Divide

**When to use:** Evaluating any marketing piece or campaign.

**Key insight:** Direct response is measurable, trackable, and accountable.
Institutional advertising is none of those things. Only DR deserves your money.
Everything else is "expensive wallpaper."

**Primary agent:** kennedy-direct-response

**Source:** No B.S. Direct Marketing

---

## MM_004: Money Pyramid

**Structure:**
```
     /\        1% Ultra-Rich
    /  \       4% Rich
   /    \      15% Comfortable
  /      \     60% Paycheck-to-Paycheck
 /________\    20% Broke
```

**When to use:** Market selection, pricing decisions, media choice.

**Key insight:** The top 20% control 80% of the wealth. Marketing to the
bottom 80% is a race to the bottom. Always consider moving UP the pyramid.

**Primary agent:** kennedy-audit, kennedy-affluent

**Source:** No B.S. Direct Marketing, Midas Touch

---

## MM_005: A-Pile / B-Pile Sort

**Formula:** Every mail piece gets sorted in 3 seconds. A-Pile or trash.

**When to use:** Any direct mail design, email subject line, first impression.

**Key insight:** Design for A-Pile FIRST: hand-addressed, real stamps,
personal look. B-Pile = bulk, corporate, window envelope. No C-Pile exists.

**Primary agent:** kennedy-media, kennedy-sales-letter

**Source:** The Ultimate Sales Letter

---

## MM_006: The Starving Crowd

**Formula:** Market selection > Everything else

**When to use:** Choosing target market, evaluating business viability.

**Key insight:** "If you have a starving crowd, you don't need a gourmet
menu. You need a hot dog stand." Find the market that is DESPERATE for
a solution, not merely interested.

**Primary agent:** kennedy-chief

**Source:** Ultimate Marketing Plan

---

## MM_007: USP as Wedge

**Formula:** "Why you vs. every other option, including doing nothing?"

**4 Pathways:** Be First | Be Best | Be Different | Be Specialized

**When to use:** Positioning, differentiation, offer creation.

**Key insight:** Without a USP, you are a commodity competing on price.
With a USP, you are the only logical choice. The USP is your wedge that
separates you from the competitive herd.

**Primary agent:** kennedy-offers

**Source:** Ultimate Marketing Plan, Chapter 2

---

## MM_008: 28-Step Sales Letter

**Formula:** Systematic, repeatable 28-step process for sales letters.

**When to use:** Writing any sales letter, landing page, or long-form copy.

**Structure:** Steps 1-6 (Preparation) + Steps 7-10 (Headline/Opening) +
Steps 11-18 (Body) + Steps 19-24 (Close) + Steps 25-28 (Review)

**Primary agent:** kennedy-sales-letter

**Source:** The Ultimate Sales Letter

---

## MM_009: 10 No B.S. Rules

**The 10 Rules:**
1. Always an offer
2. Reason to respond NOW
3. Clear instructions
4. Tracking and measurement
5. Meaningful follow-up
6. Results rule (no vanity)
7. Tough love
8. Thick skin
9. Be in the market
10. Willing to be "bad"

**When to use:** Evaluating ANY marketing piece or campaign.

**Primary agent:** kennedy-direct-response, kennedy-audit

**Source:** No B.S. Direct Marketing

---

## MM_010: Shock and Awe

**Formula:** Overwhelm with proof + value BEFORE the sale.

**Minimum 5 elements:** Welcome letter + Authority material + Testimonials +
FAQ/Objection crusher + Time-sensitive offer + Premium

**When to use:** Converting leads to customers, high-value prospect nurture.

**Key insight:** The package should make saying "no" feel foolish.

**Primary agent:** kennedy-magnetic

**Source:** Magnetic Marketing, Magnetic Marketing Course

---

## MM_011: Time Vampires

**Formula:** Protect productive time ruthlessly.

**When to use:** Productivity, meeting evaluation, opportunity filtering.

**Key insight:** Some people and activities are "time vampires" — they
consume your most productive hours and contribute nothing to results.
Identify and eliminate them without guilt.

**Primary agent:** kennedy-advisor

**Source:** Renegade Millionaire Course

---

## MM_012: Renegade Millionaire

**Formula:** Think and act contrary to conventional wisdom.

**When to use:** Strategic decisions, competitive positioning, life design.

**Key insight:** When everyone zigs, zag. The money is in doing the
opposite of the herd. Conventional wisdom is usually neither conventional
nor wise.

**Primary agent:** kennedy-advisor

**Source:** Renegade Millionaire Course, 25 Eternal Truths
