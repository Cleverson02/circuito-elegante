# Kennedy Source Guide

## Overview

This guide maps all Dan Kennedy source materials to their corresponding
frameworks, agents, and use cases. Use this when you need to trace a
recommendation back to its source or find the right material for deeper study.

---

## Books (7 Titles)

### 1. No B.S. Direct Marketing (3rd Ed, 2018)

**The foundation book.** Contains the 10 Rules, Results Triangle, Money Pyramid,
and The Great Divide. If you read only one Kennedy book, read this one.

| Framework | Chapter | Agent |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| The Great Divide (DR vs Institutional) | 1 | kennedy-direct-response |
| 10 No B.S. Rules of Direct Marketing | 2-3 | kennedy-direct-response |
| Results Triangle (Message + Market + Media) | 1, 4 | kennedy-chief |
| Money Pyramid | 5 | kennedy-audit |
| Direct Mail Mastery | 6-8 | kennedy-media |

**Best for:** Understanding WHY direct response beats institutional marketing.

---

### 2. Magnetic Marketing (2018)

**The system book.** Contains the 3-Pillar System, 9 Fatal Marketing Mistakes,
and the Shock-and-Awe Package concept. This is Kennedy's "complete marketing
system in a box."

| Framework | Chapter | Agent |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| 9 Fatal Marketing Mistakes | 1-3 | kennedy-magnetic |
| 3-Pillar System (Attract-Convert-Retain) | 4-6 | kennedy-magnetic |
| Pest-to-Welcome-Guest | 7 | kennedy-magnetic |
| Shock-and-Awe Package | 9 | kennedy-magnetic |

**Best for:** Building a COMPLETE marketing system, not just individual campaigns.

### 3. The Ultimate Sales Letter (3rd Ed, 2006)

**The copy book.** Contains the 28-Step Sales Letter System, 12 Headline Formulas,
and the A-Pile/B-Pile Framework. The definitive guide to writing direct
response sales letters.

| Framework | Chapter | Agent |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| 28-Step Sales Letter System | All | kennedy-sales-letter |
| 12 Fill-in-the-Blank Headline Formulas | 7 | kennedy-sales-letter |
| A-Pile / B-Pile Framework | Throughout | kennedy-media |
| Pest-to-Welcome-Guest Technique | 8 | kennedy-sales-letter |

**Best for:** Writing sales letters, landing pages, and long-form copy.

### 4. The Ultimate Marketing Plan (3rd Ed, 2006)

**The strategy book.** Contains USP Construction, Widget Concept, and the
Classic Mail-Order Ad Structure. Focuses on the strategic foundation.

| Framework | Chapter | Agent |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| USP Construction (4 Pathways) | 2 | kennedy-offers |

### 5. No B.S. Brand-Building by Direct Response (2014)

**The brand book.** How to build a brand that PAYS FOR ITSELF through
direct response. Contains the Winning Formula and StorySelling.

| Framework | Chapter | Agent |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| Winning Formula (DR Acquisition + Brand Retention) | 1-3 | kennedy-brand |

| Personal Branding by DR | 5-6 | kennedy-brand |

### 6. No B.S. Direct Response Social Media Marketing (2015)

**The digital book.** How to apply DR principles to social media.
Kennedy's view: social media is just another media channel — and it must
follow the same DR rules as every other channel.

| Framework | Chapter | Agent |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| DR Social Media Principles | All | kennedy-media |
| Content That Converts (not just engages) | 3-5 | kennedy-media |

**Best for:** Making social media accountable and measurable.
